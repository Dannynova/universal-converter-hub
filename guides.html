Guides on How to Make Use of Our Tools

Learn how to use each tool step by step. All guides are free, no registration required.

How to Convert PDF to Word Online – Free & Fast

Converting a PDF to an editable Word document is easy with our free tool. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to the PDF to Word converter page.
  2. Drag and drop your PDF file into the upload area, or click to select it.
  3. Wait a few seconds while the file is processed securely.
  4. Click the "Download" button to save your new Word document.

Our converter preserves text layout, tables, images, and basic formatting. Files are automatically deleted after conversion for your privacy.

How to Convert Word to PDF Online – Keep Your Original Formatting

Turn any Word document into a polished, print-ready PDF in seconds.

  1. Open the Word to PDF converter.
  2. Drop your .doc or .docx file into the upload area, or click to browse.
  3. Click "Convert to PDF" and review the live preview of your document.
  4. Click "Save as PDF", then choose "Save as PDF" in the print dialog that opens.

Fonts, tables, headings, bullet lists and images are preserved exactly as in your original Word document. Everything runs in your browser — your file never leaves your device.

How to Merge or Split PDF Files – Combine or Extract Pages Free

Combine multiple PDFs into one, or split a single PDF into separate pages.

  1. Go to the PDF Merge & Split tool.
  2. To merge: select the Merge tab, upload two or more PDFs, drag to reorder them, then click "Merge PDFs".
  3. To split: select the Split tab, upload a PDF, choose "Extract All Pages" or enter a custom range (e.g. 1-3, 5, 7-9), then click "Split PDF".
  4. Your merged or split file(s) download automatically.

You can merge up to 20 files at once. All processing happens in your browser using PDF-lib — nothing is uploaded to any server.

How to Compress Files Online – Images, PDF and ZIP

Reduce the size of images, PDFs, or bundle multiple files into a ZIP archive.

  1. Open the File Compressor.
  2. Choose a mode: Images, PDF, or ZIP Archive.
  3. Upload your file(s) by clicking the upload area or dragging files in.
  4. For images and PDFs, adjust the quality slider to control the level of compression.
  5. Click "Compress" and download your reduced-size file.

PDF compression can reduce file size by up to 90% by re-rendering pages at your chosen quality. Everything runs locally in your browser for complete privacy.

How to Edit Images Online – Crop, Resize, Add Text & More

Use our professional image editor to enhance your photos without installing software.

  1. Visit the Image Editor page.
  2. Upload an image (JPG, PNG, WEBP, or GIF).
  3. Use the toolbar to:
    • Crop or resize the image
    • Adjust brightness, contrast, saturation, or blur
    • Apply filters (grayscale, sepia, invert, vintage)
    • Add text with custom font, size, and color
    • Draw with brush or eraser
    • Remove background by selecting a color
  4. Click "Download PNG" to save your edited image.

All editing happens directly in your browser – no upload to external servers (except background removal, which is client‑side).

How to Compress Images – Reduce File Size Without Losing Quality

  1. Open the Image Compressor.
  2. Drag and drop your image (JPG, PNG, WEBP).
  3. Adjust the quality slider – lower quality gives smaller file size.
  4. Click "Compress & Download".

Use this tool to speed up your website or save storage space.

How to Generate a QR Code – For URLs, Text, or Contacts

  1. Go to the QR Code Generator.
  2. Enter your text, URL, or any information.
  3. Click "Generate QR Code".
  4. Download the QR code as PNG and use it anywhere.

Ideal for business cards, flyers, social media, and product packaging.

How to Create a Strong Password – Secure Random Password Generator

  1. Visit the Password Generator.
  2. Choose the password length (4–32 characters).
  3. Select which character types to include (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols).
  4. Click "Generate Password".
  5. Copy the password to your clipboard.

Use this to create unique, hack‑resistant passwords for your accounts.

How to Convert Text to Speech – Listen to Any Text

  5. Click "Stop" to end early.

Great for proofreading, language learning, or accessibility.

How to Convert Units – Length, Weight, Volume, Temperature, Area, Speed, Data, Pressure

  1. Open the Unit Converter.
  2. Select the category (e.g., length, weight).

  4. Choose the target unit.
  5. Click "Convert" to see the result.

Accurate conversions for everyday and scientific use.

How to Calculate Land Area – Plots, Acres, Hectares, Square Meters

  1. Go to the Land Calculator.
  2. Use one of the options:
    • Standard Plot Sizes: see pre‑calculated 50×100 ft and 60×120 ft plots.
    • Custom Plot Size: enter length and width in feet.
    • Convert Area to Plots: enter area in m², hectares, or acres.
    • Acres to Plots + Metric: convert acres to plots, hectares, and m².
  3. Click the corresponding "Calculate" or "Convert" button.

Essential for real estate, farming, and property development.

How to Convert DWG to KMZ – Create Google Earth Markers

  1. Open the DWG to KMZ converter.
  2. Upload your DWG file (or leave empty to use manual coordinates).
  3. Select the UTM zone, enter easting/northing, and choose datum (WGS84, Minna, etc.).
  4. Click "Generate KMZ Marker".
  5. Download the KMZ file and open it in Google Earth.

Note: This creates a location marker. For full geometry conversion, use desktop GIS software.

How to Convert Currency – Live Exchange Rates, Time Zone & Date Difference

  3. For time zone: select two cities, click "Show Times".
  4. For date difference: pick two dates, click "Calculate".

Rates are updated daily. Perfect for travellers, freelancers, and global teams.

How to Convert NNO, UTM and WGS84 Coordinates – For Surveyors & Engineers

Convert between Nigerian local survey coordinates, UTM, WGS84 (GPS) and Minna Datum.

  1. Visit the Coordinate Converter.
  2. Choose a conversion mode from the tabs: NNO→UTM/WGS84, UTM→NNO, WGS84→UTM, UTM→WGS84, WGS84→Minna, or Minna→WGS84.
  3. For NNO conversions, search and select your local origin (e.g. Abeokuta, Ijebu-Ode, Sagamu) from the Ogun State origin table.
  4. Enter your coordinate values and select the output system.
  5. Click "Convert" to see your result in metres and decimal degrees, with a copy button for each value.

Supports all 55+ Ogun State local origins, the Minna Datum (West/Mid/East belts and UTM Zones 31N/32N), and global WGS84 coordinates used by GPS devices. All calculations run instantly in your browser — no data is ever sent to a server.
